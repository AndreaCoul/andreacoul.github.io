What to Pack

🏕️ Camping Gear

  • Tent - Reach out if you need one
  • Sleeping bag
  • Sleeping pad, air mattress, or tent cot
  • Pillow
  • Flashlight or headlamp
  • Camp chair for around the fire - Reach out if you need one
  • Cooler

🎒 Other Essentials

  • Bible/Notebook/Pen
  • Water bottle
  • Bugspray - the ticks are no joke this year!
  • Cards/Games/Football/Etc. (optional)
  • Toiletries (toothbrush/paste/etc.)

👕 Clothing Reminder

  • Sweatshirt or hoodie (as it can get cool at night even in the summer)
  • Rain jacket (for those pop-up showers)
